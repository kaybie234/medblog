# MediTech Pulse

A simple medical blog site for sharing medical facts, teaching health literacy, and covering daily news in medicine and technology.

## Files

- `index.html` — blog homepage with sections for facts, lessons, and news
- `styles.css` — responsive blog styling
- `app.js` — data-driven rendering for facts, lessons, and news

## How to use

Open `index.html` in a browser to view the site.

## Content focus

- Medical facts and evidence-based insights
- Practical teaching and health literacy guidance
- Daily updates on medical research, digital health, and technology innovations
